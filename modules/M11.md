# M11 — Route protection + access gate

Track: Auth · Depends: M10 · Blocks: M13, M16, M33 · Agent time: ~2h

## Goal
Nothing private is reachable without a session, and you control who gets in.

## Files you may touch
- `src/middleware.ts`
- `src/lib/auth/guards.ts`
- `src/app/(app)/layout.tsx`
- `src/app/(app)/pending/page.tsx`

## Spec

**Middleware**
- `(app)/**` → session + `emailVerified` required, else → `/login?next=<path>`
- `/admin/**` → session + `role === 'ADMIN'`, else → **404**. Not 403. Admin routes should not admit they exist.
- `(marketing)/**`, `(auth)/**` → public
- Logged in and hitting `/login` or `/register` → `/dashboard`

**Access gate** — `accessStatus`:
- `PENDING` → `/pending`: *"You're on the list. We're opening access in small groups. You'll get an email."* Not an error. A door, not a wall.
- `ACTIVE` → through
- `REVOKED` → `/pending` with different copy

**This is your payment system for v0.1.** Someone pays you by UPI, you flip a switch in `/admin/users` (M33). Twenty users. Correct amount of engineering for twenty users — and it means no company registration, no Razorpay mandates, no RBI e-mandate rules, before anyone has proven they'll pay at all.

**`guards.ts`** — `requireSession()`, `requireActive()`, `requireAdmin()` for use inside route handlers. Middleware protects pages; guards protect APIs. **Both. Not one.**

## Out of scope
Payments. Trials. Subscriptions. Roles beyond LEARNER and ADMIN.
