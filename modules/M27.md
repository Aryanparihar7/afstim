## Spec

**The whole page:**
```
eyebrow  SHIP YOUR FIRST APP · 3 OF 10 VERIFIED

Card
  eyebrow  MISSION 04 · 25 MIN
  h2       Deploy to Vercel
  p        Get your app onto a public URL that anyone in the world can open.
  [Continue mission]

           A thin progress bar. --border-strong track, --text fill. No percentage label.
```

**That is the entire dashboard.** Your PRD §22 lists nine things "visible within five seconds" — reputation, builder level, achievements, recent activity, statistics, recommended next step. Nine things visible in five seconds is nine things competing, and the learner reads none of them.

There is exactly **one** thing they should do next, and we know what it is. Show that.

**Nav** — `AFSTIM` (mono, → dashboard) · `Journey` · user menu (Settings / CLI / Log out). Six items max. No search, no notifications bell, no reputation counter.

**Empty state** — no missions started:
```
h2   Ship your first app
p    Ten missions. About twelve hours. You'll end with a live URL you built.
     Mission 1 takes about 20 minutes. Start when you have a clear hour.
[Start mission 1]
```

**Journey complete:**
```
h2   You shipped it.
p    Your app is live at <their URL>. You built it, deployed it, and you can do it again.
[Tell me what to build next]  → /feedback
```
No certificate. No badge. The Product Bible: *"The final outcome is confidence. Not certificates."* Hold that line.

## Out of scope
Reputation. Builder Level. Streaks. Achievements. Recent activity feed. Statistics. Charts. Multiple journeys. Notifications. Search. **None of these are in v0.1. Do not add one "since it's easy."**

## Founder gate

2. Squint at the screen. The one thing you should do is still obvious. ☐
3. Fresh account → the empty state makes you want to start. ☐
4. Nothing on this page is a number that goes up. (If reputation appeared, revert it.) ☐
