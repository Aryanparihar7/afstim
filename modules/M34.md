## Spec

**`/admin/events`**

Top — **the funnel**, and nothing above it:
```
Accounts created            42
Verified email              31   (74%)
Started mission 1           24   (77%)
Verified mission 1          19   (79%)
Verified mission 4          11   (58%)   ← THE NUMBER
Verified mission 10          3   (27%)
```

**Mission 4 is where the promise is kept.** It's the first live public URL. If under 50% of mission-1 starters reach mission 4, missions 1–4 are wrong — fix the content, do not add features. Label it in the UI so you can't forget.

Below — raw events table, filter by type, last 200, paginated.

**`/admin/feedback`** — list, newest first. Category · message · email · date. Filter by category. `[Mark read]` (add `readAt` to `Feedback`, migration `add_feedback_read`).

**No charts.** Numbers in a table. You have 20 users; a line chart of 20 points is decoration.

## Out of scope
Cohorts. Retention curves. Third-party analytics — **no PostHog, no GA, no Mixpanel, no Sentry.** Every one of them is a monthly bill and a privacy surface for data we already have in Postgres.
