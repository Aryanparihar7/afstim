# M06 — Landing page

Track: Marketing · Depends: M02 · Blocks: M39 · Agent time: ~4h

## Goal
A stranger reads the top of this page and can say what Afstim does and who it's for, in one sentence, in under ten seconds.

## Files you may touch
- `src/app/(marketing)/page.tsx`
- `src/app/(marketing)/layout.tsx`
- `src/components/afstim/marketing/**`

## Spec

**Structure — exactly this, in this order. Nothing added.**

```
Nav        AFSTIM (mono eyebrow, left) · [Start the journey] (right). Nothing else.

Hero       eyebrow  AFSTIM
           display  You can code. You've never shipped.
           sub      Ten missions. Your own editor. A live URL with your name on it.
           [Start the journey]  [See the missions]

SIGNATURE  A <Terminal> showing a real check run, rows resolving on scroll-in:
             $ npx afstim check
             ▸ Mission 4 — Deploy to Vercel
               ✓ Your app builds without errors
               ✓ Your URL responds from the public internet
               ✓ HTTPS is working with a valid certificate
               Mission verified.

Outcome    "What you'll have at the end" — 6 items, mono list:
           A live URL · HTTPS · A real database · Environment variables in production
           A deploy pipeline · A repo you can show someone

Missions   "The ten missions" — numbered 01–10, mono, title + one line each.
           Numbering is correct here because it IS a sequence.

How        Three columns: Your editor · Our checks · A mentor who knows the mission.
           One sentence each. No icons.

Who        "This is for you if" — honest, uncomfortable, specific:
           You've finished courses but never shipped.
           You have projects on GitHub that nobody can open.
           You've never bought a domain, and you're not sure what DNS is.

FAQ        6 questions. Real ones. Including "Is this just another course?" and
           "Why do I need this if I have ChatGPT?" — answer both honestly.

Footer     Afstim · Feedback · Terms · Privacy
```

**The signature is the terminal.** It is the most characteristic thing in this product's world — the moment a check goes green is why anyone pays. Everything else on the page stays silent so it lands.

**Motion:** rows resolve `pending → pass` on scroll-in, staggered 200ms. That is the *only* animation on the page. Honour `prefers-reduced-motion` — reduced = all rows already passed on load.

**Copy rules:** sentence case. Active voice. No "revolutionise", "empower", "journey to mastery", "unlock your potential". Say the true thing plainly.

**Do not fabricate:** no testimonials, no "trusted by 500 students", no company logos, no star ratings, no fake counters. We have zero users. Saying otherwise is the fastest way to be exactly the thing we're replacing.

## Out of scope
Pricing page. Signup form (M09 owns it — the buttons link to `/register`). Blog. Animations beyond the one. Any illustration.

## Done when
- [ ] tsc, lint, build pass
- [ ] 375px wide: nothing overflows, nothing overlaps
- [ ] Lighthouse accessibility ≥ 95
- [ ] Zero layout shift on font load

## Founder gate
1. **Show it to 3 people who don't know what you're building.** Ask: *"what does this do and who is it for?"* If any of them can't answer in one sentence, the hero is wrong. Fix it before M07. ☐
2. On your phone, the terminal is readable. ☐
3. Reduced motion on → nothing moves, page still makes sense. ☐
4. Read every sentence aloud. Any that sounds like marketing, cut. ☐
