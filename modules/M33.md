## Spec

**Shell** — same tokens, no marketing nav. Links: Users · Events · Feedback · AI. Visually plainer than the learner app — you should always know which one you're looking at.

**`/admin/users`** — table: email · joined · verified? · access · mission · verified count · last active. Search by email. Filter by access status.

**Access toggle** — a `<select>`: PENDING / ACTIVE / REVOKED. Changing → confirm dialog naming the person and the change. Writes an `Event` (`admin_access_changed`) with the actor's id.

**This is the payment system.** Someone pays you by UPI, you set ACTIVE. Twenty users. That's the correct amount of engineering for twenty users, and it means **no company registration, no Razorpay integration, no RBI e-mandate compliance, before a single person has proven they'll pay.**

When you have 200 users and this hurts, that pain is a good problem and you'll have revenue to solve it with.

**Cannot do, in v0.1:** impersonate a learner (PRD §68 forbids it), edit progress, delete a user, read reflections in the UI (Prisma Studio if you must — and notice that you want to; that's a signal about content).

## Out of scope
